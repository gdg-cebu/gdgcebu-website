About GDG Cebu

Google Developers Group (GDG) Cebu is a non-profit group of amateurs and professionals (technology users, specialists, and developers) who are independent from Google but are enthusiasts and daily users of Google technologies. Members meet and socialize on a regular basis.

GDG Cebu is an independent group; the activities and opinions expressed by the group should in no way be linked to Google, the corporation.

The group was organized to provide an avenue for:

  1. learning about up-to-date Google tools for personal and professional use (for free!)
  2. networking opportunities
  3. growing local and international connections
  4. professionals to help others and share insights on cutting-edge technology
  5. sharing success stories of individuals who have benefited from being a part of the group

Should you have further questions, please feel free to contact us at gdgcebulead@googlegroups.com.
